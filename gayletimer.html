GAYLE TIMER



Gayle Timer

As part of training for my first full-time role at Cigna, we participated in a design thinking challenge - design a problem to a solution in just an hour. I interviewed my friend Gayle about her typical morning routine, and I found that she often ends up losing track of time and being late. Thus, I sketched up some quick wireframes of a timer application catered specifically to each individual morning routine for the design challenge. I took those initial ideas to work towards creating a high-fidelity prototype of this timer application, and I named the project Gayle Timer.

THE ISSUE

Most people have a morning routine that they follow, but many struggle with managing the limited amount of time they have in the morning.

This results in being rushed, forgetting things, or being late.

THE SOLUTION

An easy-to-use timer application would be a useful tool for those who have busy morning routines to have on their mobile phone.

- There will be a timer for each task in the individual's morning routine.

- The time remaining adjusts if you finish a task early or late.

- The time remaining appears clearly and constantly on the home screen.
