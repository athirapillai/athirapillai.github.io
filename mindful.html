MINDFUL



Mindful: Simply Affordable Medication

As an intern at Cigna, I was a part of the Summer Innovation project.
Each group of interns was given a problem in healthcare to attempt to solve with the creation of a web app. My team was given the task to eliminate surprise pharmacy costs. Over the course of twelve weeks, we planned, analyzed, designed, developed, and presented our solution: Mindful.

THE ISSUE

Prescription drugs are unaffordable for many Americans.
Often, a patient goes to their pharmacy to pick up their prescription and is alarmed at the cost of their co-pay.

This is unfortunately a common issue within the healthcare industry, and many people struggle to pay for their important prescriptions.

THE SOLUTION

At the very beginning of the planning phase, we brainstormed ideas on how we can keep Cigna’s customers aware of how much they will pay before they go to the pharmacy.

- They can search for pharmacies near their location and choose the cheapest option.
- They can select the generic option instead of brand if it is available.
- View coverage details right from the web app.
- Have a “My Prescriptions” feature that has patient prescriptions at selected locations with real-time price updates.
- Be notified of checking prescription costs whenever users are currently at a pharmacy or doctor’s office.
- Integrated portal within the existing MyCigna app for users to easily find.

DESIGN STANDARDS

It was important to follow Cigna’s design standards for this project, from Cigna colors to Cigna typography.
