PRANTL'S BAKERY



Prantl's Bakery

This website re-design was my capstone project. We followed the Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC) to develop this project. As the Design lead, my duties included leading the team in creating the overall design of the new website, creating prototypes of website pages, and conducting usability testing on these prototypes to ensure they are user friendly.

THE ISSUE

Prantl's Bakery is home to the "Greatest Cake in America", but the current Prantl’s Bakery website is difficult to use and not mobile-friendly.

The website’s issues cause its users to have trouble navigating through the website’s pages, ordering bakery goods, and viewing specific details for potential holiday or wedding orders.

THE SOLUTION

Our team will create a user friendly, modern website for Prantl's Bakery.


- We will have the client select one of the multiple platforms available to us to create a responsive, e-commerce website to boost ease of online ordering.
- Selecting an online website-making platform will allow the client to easily go back and update any information.
