KARASCH



Karasch & Associates

As an intern at Matrix Web Design, my primary project was to re-design the outdated website for Karasch & Associates, a local law firm.

THE ISSUE

Karasch & Associates had an outdated, difficult-to-use, and badly designed website.

As a quickly growing company, they had trouble keeping up with their customers' needs, and their overall company branding needed a boost.

THE SOLUTION

Karasch & Associates need a new, upgraded website that is easy to use for their customers, responsive on all devices, easy for their team to go back and make changes, as well as aesthetically pleasing.

- A WordPress website will allow the team to easily make changes whenever they would like.
- A WordPress template from TemplateMonster will enable the team to contact quick live support for any specific template editing issues that may occur in the future.
