ABOUT ME



Hi there!

I’m Athira, and I am a student at the Pennsylvania State University, graduating this May. I’m double majoring in Information Sciences & Technology (IST) and Security & Risk Analysis (SRA).

My background in IST has provided me with a passion for front-end design, for both aesthetic and usability, while my background in SRA has given me awareness on security practices that I can incorporate into design.

In my free time, I love to paint and draw. I enjoy travelling to new places and exploring cities, and I love to spend time with friends.
